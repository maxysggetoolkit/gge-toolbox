🏰 Fortress Tracker

NPC fortresses on the map — Barbarian, Desert and Dragon forts. Each fort has a 24h cooldown after anyone attacks it. Enter your name below and the "Attackable" timer becomes your timer — for forts you last hit, it counts the longer 72h personal cooldown instead. "Last attacker" is who hit it most recently, not an owner.

Data: gge-tracker.com public API.
