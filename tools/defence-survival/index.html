🛡️ Defence Survival Calculator

The defender's-seat companion to the Wall-Break sim. Set your castellan, garrison and defensive tools for each wall segment, then the attack coming at you — and see whether each segment holds and whether your Keep falls. Resolved with Goodgame's own per-wave battle maths.

⚠️ Model note. The combat engine mirrors the public GeneralsCamp battle simulator's formula (the community standard) and uses its unit/tool values. It's a close model, not a guarantee — exact in-game numbers shift with patches. If a real defence report disagrees, send it to Maxy and the constants get tuned.
👑 Your castellan & castle

Protection comes off your castle screen (wall / gate / moat %). The castellan adds defence strength and abilities — defaulting to a Sasaki-style setup as an example.

⚔️ Garrison & defensive tools

Defenders per segment (melee hold the line, ranged shoot from the wall) plus the tools on that segment. Front has a gate; flanks are wall-only; everything spare sits in the Keep. Defaults are max-level sovereign (mead-fed) defenders.

🔥 Incoming attack

The commander's bonuses, the waves, and what's thrown at each segment. Rams cut your gate, breaching tools cut your wall, shields sap your ranged defenders, support tools hit the Keep.

📋 Verdict

How it resolves. Each wall segment is fought wave by wave with Goodgame's formula: melee and ranged are matched in separate lanes, the weaker side of a lane is wiped while the winner loses (weaker/stronger)^1.5 of its force, and wall/gate/moat protection multiplies your defenders' strength. Your garrison persists and grinds down across the waves; a segment breaches when it's emptied, and the survivors pour into the Keep for the final stand. Your castle falls only if the Keep is overrun.
