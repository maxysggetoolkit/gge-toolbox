⚖️ Value weights — tune what matters (drives the 🏆 Overall ranking; troops & tools are already weighted by combat power)
Value model: troops & tools are scored by combat power (qty × atk/def), so a pile of weak units no longer beats a smaller strong pack. Other categories score by quantity. The ⚖️ weights above let you say what you care about (e.g. crank Resources for mead, drop mid-troops) — that drives the 🏆 Overall ranking. There's no in-game data for cross-category worth, so this is your call, not a guess. Prices: exact where the offer's tier matches a known AU pack ($8.99 / $17.99 / $34.99 …), estimated (marked ≈) for the cheaper bundle tiers. Value is how much of your chosen reward you get per A$; the +% badge is the game's own value rating. “Newest era” sorts by the game's internal offer ID — a rough recency proxy that pushes current-meta bundles (Affluence, Offerings, Sasaki) up and retired seasonal ones down. The static data holds the offer definitions, not your live daily rotation or the very newest amounts, so search by what you actually see in-game (now that names match — “Affluence tickets”, “Offering to Beatrice”, etc.).
