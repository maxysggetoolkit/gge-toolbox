📅 The daily-bundle chain (your daily rotation)

The Daily Bundles tab runs a 7-step chain — each consecutive day the bonus climbs. Days 1–6 cost A$17.99, day 7 jumps to A$89.99 for the big payout. The chain is where the best ruby rates in the whole shop live.

⏳ The win-back ladder — when the game pays you to wait

From the game's own offer-targeting data: special one-time bundles unlock purely by how long it's been since your last purchase. Same pack, fatter bundle the longer you lapse.

How to use this: if you buy regularly, you'll never see the big tiers. Planning a purchase anyway? Check your days-lapsed below first — being patient past day 61 or 121 multiplies the freebies several times over. Offers are also gated by level, lifetime spend, your last-90-days spend, and even your current ruby stock (running low changes what you're shown).
