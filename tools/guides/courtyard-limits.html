🏯 Courtyard Limits

Defence Courtyard Reference ~5 min read

Only troops within your courtyard limit actually fight — and the limit comes from more places than most players realise. Here is every source: the three classic buildings, the Reinforced Vault, defence decorations and construction items, with the caps that govern them. Data: June 2026.

How the limits work

Two separate limits apply in the courtyard:

  • Personal yard limit — caps your own defenders in the CY.
  • Alliance yard limit — caps support troops from alliance mates.

Note the Keep quirk: levels 6–7 raise your personal limit while the alliance limit stalls at 200k — it only climbs again (to 250k) at level 8.

Defence decorations

30 decorations carry a "Bonus to courtyard defense troop capacity" effect — mostly the Exalted Red event-deco line (Red = defence, Green = economy, Purple = recruitment). These bonuses are capped across all your decos:

Deco caps: +175,000 personal CY capacity · +150,000 alliance support capacity · +160% wall capacity · +130% CY defence strength.

Construction items

A handful of temporary construction items add courtyard capacity. They're event-season skins with effects baked in — a top-up, not a foundation. No permanent primary or relic build item grants CY capacity.
