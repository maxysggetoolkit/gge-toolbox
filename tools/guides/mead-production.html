🍺 Mead Optimisation

Optimisation Economy Sovereign Troops ~6 min read

Mead is what keeps your Sovereign troops alive — the elite units unlocked through the Hall of Legends. Four of the eight sovereign unit types drink mead (the other four eat beef), and if a castle runs out, those soldiers die. Mead isn't farmed directly: your Relic Brewery converts food + honey into mead, so optimising mead really means optimising a three-resource chain.

How the chain works

  • Relic Brewery (unlock: Hall of Legends legendary level 45, "Master Brewer"). One per castle, 5×5. Converts 5 food + 3 honey → 1 mead at base ratio — upgrading the brewery improves the ratio, so levels pay twice (more output and cheaper input). 30 levels since the March 2024 update.
  • The slider: the brewery has a % slider — lower it to produce less mead and consume less food/honey. Use it to throttle the drain when your food balance is struggling.
  • Honey comes from Apiaries (unlock: legendary level 40, "Beekeeper") — up to 15 per castle, 5×5 each.
  • Food feeds the brewery too. Every point of mead production leans on your food economy — a weak food setup caps your mead no matter how good the brewery is. Do the food optimisation alongside this.
  • Net mead per hour = production − sovereign troop consumption. Negative net drains the storehouse; at zero stored mead, sovereign troops start dying.

The optimisation checklist — buildings

  • Relic Brewery → level 14–15 as the efficient stopping point; push to 30 in one go if you have the resources banked (each level also improves the food/honey ratio).
  • Relic Mead Distillery → level 8 (construction-token building, boosts mead).
  • Barrel Workshop → level 6 (construction-token building, mead production).
  • Honey Gardens → level 6 (construction-token building, honey production).
  • Apiaries — build them out and pair with the relic honey production build item (level 1 is the value pick; upgrading both is whale territory).
  • Granaries → level 30 and 2× Relic Greenhouse per castle → level 20 — this is the food side that feeds the brewery. Relic Conservatories ×2 only if you spend big.
  • Buy the Mead District — it lets you tuck the mead buildings away off your castle grid, freeing plots for decorations.

The optimisation checklist — boosts

  • Overseers (Kings Market, weekly): Mead 563 rubies, Honey 563, Food 157. Cheap for what they give — set a reminder so they don't lapse.
  • Coat of arms: the +20% mead emblem is the standout; 15% mead or 15–20% food variants are also viable.
  • Research (Hall of Legends): Northman Tankards = +20% mead, Honey Pots lv 5 = +20% honey, Three-Field Crop Rotation lv 5 = +20% food.
  • Castellan hero with a mead % bonus — but only if the hero's defence effects are still good. A castle that gets wiped because of a bad castellan loses you more than the production gain. Defence first, production second.
  • Public-order decorations: place your highest-PO decos where they cover the most production. If your mead buildings are fully built in outposts, the boost lands bigger there than in the main castle. Golden rule — your best decos should never sit in inventory.
  • Districts: buy the military/trade districts and level them, always moving the biggest buildings in first — every plot freed is deco space.
  • Build items (Beyond the Horizon): the brewery primary slot goes +70 mead at lv 1 → +1,350 at lv 30; the relic brewery slot is the monster — +10,200 at lv 1 → +15,000 at lv 30. Honey items: primary +30 → +418 (lv 25), relic +2,200 → +7,000 (lv 25). Top BTH rewards also include temp honey/mead items.
  • Hall expansions + Berimond titles — more castle space = more decorations. Delete buildings you don't need.

Priorities if you're starting from zero

  • 1. Unlock the chain: Beekeeper (leg. 40) → apiaries; Master Brewer (leg. 45) → brewery.
  • 2. Brewery to 14–15, apiaries built, granaries/greenhouses up — the structural base.
  • 3. Cheap permanent boosts: research lines, coat of arms, CT buildings (distillery 8 / barrel workshop 6 / honey gardens 6).
  • 4. Weekly upkeep: the three overseers.
  • 5. Long game: relic brewery build item, PO deco quality, district levels, brewery → 30.

Rule of thumb: the brewery ratio means every mead is really ~5 food + 3 honey — so a mead problem is usually a food or honey problem in disguise. Fix the chain in that order: food base → honey supply → brewery level → % boosts.
