🌌 Outer Realms

Event Temp server Level 70+ Strategy ~6 min read

Outer Realms drops every level-70+ castle lord in the world onto one fresh server, with nothing but a starting castle. It rotates bi-weekly with Beyond the Horizon — and right now it's the only place to get the food district item.

How it works

You enter with a chosen starting castle and build from scratch — troops, tools and resources from your main account do not come with you. When the event ends the server is wiped and nothing comes back, including rubies — only your event ranking rewards pay out. Daily rankings are multiplied and rolled into the overall ranking each day, so consistent play beats one big day.

Why play it

  • The food district item is currently only obtainable in Outer Realms.
  • It's one of the best sources of construction & upgrade tokens in the game.

Per-event reward tracks and their exclusive decorations: coming to this guide soon.

The three variants

Snappy SwapBeat a higher-ranked player and you swap ranks with them. Ranks recalculate daily at 00:30 CET.
Masters of MightMight points from constructing and upgrading buildings.
Reading older guides? The Misty Castle and Crossplay variants no longer exist. If a wiki or video presents them as current, treat the rest of its Outer Realms info with suspicion too.

Same logic as Beyond the Horizon: the big-ruby castle is for staying compact and defending itself; if you're grinding, take a cheap castle and build. Remember the rubies you spend here are gone for good — budget accordingly.

Core strategy (F2P-friendly)

  1. Join or form an alliance immediately — support protects your troops while you're offline and unlocks group play.
  2. Farm robber barons near you from day 1: coins, resources and equipment drops. Build commanders from high melee/range drops, then climb to higher-level RBCs and player targets.
  3. Recruit macemen + crossbowmen early — the fastest useful pairing (75 speed).
  4. Day 4–5 milestone: ~65,000 coins buys the Legacy of the Goat Cult commander (90/90) from the Equipment Trader — the single biggest free power spike. Limit: two purchases per set per event.
  5. Spending? The trader also sells the Apparatus of the Summoner's Apprentice (60k rubies) and Artifacts of the New Bloom (30k rubies).
  6. In Heritage Hunter, protect your points — you bleed ~10% per lost defence. Don't sit fat and offline.
