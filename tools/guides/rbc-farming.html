🏰 RBC Farming

Robber Barons Resources Rubies Routine ~4 min read

Robber Baron Castles (RBCs) only level up when you attack them — and the higher they go, the better they pay. Levelled-up barons are a steady engine for coins, resources, equipment and rubies. Here's how to farm them efficiently.

Why level your RBCs

Each attack levels a baron a little, and higher levels drop more coins, resources and better equipment — plus the occasional ruby and time skip. On top of the per-hit loot:

  • Relic shards questline: 300 relic shards for every 10 maxed RBCs destroyed.
  • Grand Tournament quests: "hit X towers" tasks pay a few thousand rubies a day if you ride the cooldowns — and give every type of time skip.
  • Steady food: run your food slightly negative and let RBC loot cover the deficit.

A good target is at least 20 maxed RBCs in each kingdom.

The outer kingdoms start at a higher level, which is why they need far fewer hits to max. Every level has sub-levels — several victories per level-up — which is where those hit counts come from.

Mechanics

  • Cooldown: about 3 hours between attacks on the same RBC.
  • No RBC count limit. Level as many barons as you like — there's no cap on how many you can farm.
  • Daily attack cap: roughly 3,500 attacks per day (advisor attacks excluded). This is the real ceiling on a day's grind — not a per-RBC limit.
  • RBCs spawn randomly on the map; loot scales with level (coins, food, wood, stone, the kingdom resource, and equipment).

The routine

  1. Pick a farm set close to your castles and outposts — as many barons as you want.
  2. Hit each one on its 3-hour timer. Save attack presets so reloading is one tap.
  3. Run food slightly negative; the loot covers it.
  4. Stack Grand Tournament tower quests on top for the daily ruby payout.
  5. Cash the 300-shard quest every 10 maxed RBCs.
  6. If you're a heavy grinder, keep half an eye on the ~3,500 attacks/day ceiling.
