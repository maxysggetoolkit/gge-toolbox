🧱 Wall & Courtyard Defence Caps

Add up your courtyard defender capacity, wall capacity % and courtyard defence strength from your buildings and defence decorations — and see exactly how close each is to the game's hard cap (so you stop wasting decos past it).

Buildings — courtyard capacity

Defence decorations

How the caps work: building courtyard capacity stacks freely; the decoration bonuses are what's capped — +175,000 personal CY troop capacity, +160% wall capacity and +130% courtyard defence strength across all your decos combined. Anything past a cap is wasted. Building tables and caps are in-game verified (June 2026); deco values come from the game data cache. Alliance support capacity shown is from buildings only.
