🌀 Rift Raid Points

Work out how many points your hits score and how many it takes to qualify for the Boss Defeat reward. Points = the share of an area's troops you defeat × that area's multiplier. A full courtyard clear scores the whole courtyard multiplier; the courtyard is worth 10× the wall.
