⏱️ Timer Board

Track every build, research, recruitment and travel timer in one place — tagged by account or teammate. Live countdowns, the exact finish time, a browser alert when it's ready, and optional Discord pings. Stored on this device.

Quick add:
🪝 Discord pings ▸
Route specific people to their own channels (optional)
Add a person on a timer first, then their row appears here to paste a per-person webhook.
Your webhook URL is a secret — anyone who has it can post to that channel. It's saved only on this device and pings go straight from your browser to Discord; nothing passes through this site. For a guaranteed ping when you're away (tab closed), you'd need an always-on sender — that's what the local bot is for.

Your timers

Timers and your Discord settings are saved in this browser's local storage on this device — they survive a refresh but aren't synced anywhere. Countdowns keep running off the saved finish time even with the tab closed.
