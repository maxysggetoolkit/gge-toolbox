📊 Every level with this hit

How it works: your strength = (ranged troops × ranged strength × ranged malus + melee troops × melee strength × melee malus) × (1 + commander CY% + tools + the fixed +60% courtyard-assault bonus) + Giant Slayer. Per-troop strength already bakes in your troop boosts (calibrated default 2,440 for an L10 mead-fed elite — tweak it under Advanced). The boss's strength = its real CY units × their defence × (1 + its CY defence bonus) — dormant spores and rift-eggs add zero defence (proven via Giant Slayer maths) but still count toward the kill total. If you out-muscle the boss you clear the whole courtyard and losses follow the (boss ÷ you)1.5 curve; if not, your wave wipes and kills only (you ÷ boss)1.5 of the real spores. Fungal is calibrated on 10 reports across L10/L11/L12 (rms 13%); Ashen on 1 report; Necromancer is uncalibrated — and watch its malus: it cuts ranged at L11 and melee at L12+ by 60%.
