✓ Validated against real Level 11 Fungal stage-1 and stage-2 clears: garrisons, boss effects (+10,000% wall/gate, +10,100% front/flank, +350% courtyard) and courtyard health all match the game. Calibration finding — a heavily-tooled top hit reduces boss protection to ~900% net (not zero), hence the multi-wave grind.

Tools: the gate tool (ram) feeds the front reduction, the wall tool (ladder) feeds both flanks; extra gear reduction adds on top. Commander: attack +% boosts wave strength, defender −% weakens the garrison, kill % removes a flat slice every wave. Dummy waves run first with a token force — near-zero losses but they still fire the kill %. Counts are per segment (the army you commit to each lane).

Model: attacker strength = count × unit attack × (1 + bonuses − boss malus); defender strength = garrison × defence stat × (1 + boss front/flank strength) × (1 − commander reduction) ÷ (1 + your front/flank gear) × (1 + max(0, protection − tool − gear reduction)); melee/ranged lanes resolve by attacker share; losses follow (weaker/stronger)1.5; waves hit the surviving garrison sequentially. A breach opens the courtyard window. Points = kill share × wall point factor.
